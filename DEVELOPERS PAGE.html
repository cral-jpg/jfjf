Explore Different Endings

To uncover all the endings in Cozybrook Chronicles, here's a cheat sheet that will guide you to the best and worst choices:

  • Ending 1 - Full Restoration Ending: Help the town thrive by making morally good decisions. Always choose to help others and prioritize the greater good. Win 3 minigames to get this ending.
  • Ending 2 - No Restoration Ending: Refuse to win any minigames and disregard the well-being of others. This will lead to the town's downfall. Win no minigames to get this ending.
  • Ending 3 - Partial Restoration Ending: A mix of good and bad choices will lead to a balanced outcome where the town survives, but some sacrifices were made. Win 1 or 2 minigames to get this ending.

Use this guide to experiment with different decisions and unlock all the outcomes in the game!
